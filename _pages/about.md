I am a PhD candidate in Economics at Bordeaux School of Economics (BxSE). My research lies at the intersection of international trade, economic history, and conflict economics, with a particular focus on the effects of armed conflicts on trade patterns and competitiveness, and on the role of strategic goods in shaping international economic relations.
